History of Anno

Anno Museum is a new museum located in Taipei.
The museum was founded in 2015, by six students in
TIBAMI who have a lot of enthusiasm for art and life.
They hope that through the establishment of the museum, education, and culture can be promoted so that more people can know and getclose to art.
Without setting limits on style, they want to show a
wide range of artworks from classic to trendy for the
general public.
